FAQ

Is 200% volume safe?

Use appropriate volume levels. Prolonged high volume may affect hearing.

Can I use it without Dropbox?

Local playback works. Cloud streaming requires Dropbox.

What formats are supported?

MP3/WAV/M4A/FLAC and more.

Where are ads shown?

Ads are not shown on the playback screen. Frequency is limited.

Where is my data stored?

Settings/tokens are stored on-device. No external personal data storage.

Can children use the app?

Under 13 may use only with parental/guardian consent.
